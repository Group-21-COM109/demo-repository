Our Journey

Our journey to your favourite florist

2022

Where It All Began

In the beginning it was just an idea, a small business based around a natrual flora experience, founded by locals for locals.

2023

Business Is Booming

By 2023 our small local business had started to gain traction eventually leading us to hit a big milestone of over 100 orders placed. From this point forward we knew that we had stumbled upon something special that could be expanded to even greater heights.

2024

Another location

In 2024 we became more than just a local small business, we began brainstorming expansion ideas until we landed on what we needed most, another location. With orders and customer demand at an all time high we decided that covering new ground was the move we needed to reach a wider audience than we already had.

2025

Going Online

This online website should have came sooner, we were unaware of the amount of online shoppers that we could appeal to reducing the travelling customers had to do to reach our stores. As a result of this website the positive feedback has been immense with the ability to order our floral products directly to your doorstep along with plenty of information about each of our products so you are informed of what you are buying.

2026

Present day

That brings us to today, with over 1000 orders placed online alone we have reached new heights that never would of seemed possible to us as a small local business in 2022. Now with a worldwide customer base we only plan to go further from here by imrpoving the user experience even more. Becuase to us, our customer's experience is our number one priority here at Bustling And Blooming Flowers Co.
